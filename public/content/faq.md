# FAQ

##### For Brands

### 1. What is growth marketing?

Growth marketing is the greatest thing since canned bread.  

Imagine having a small army of passionate, raving customers telling their friends and family about your products.

**Except, they have 100,000 friends each.**  

With growth marketing, every interaction with customers goes further. We'll take what you're doing organically with social media, refit it for scale, and put your product in front of the world. 
Next to it, we'll set up touchpoints to make sure your prospective customers are learning about you and falling in love with your brand.

We work with you to create opportunities to amplify your business. We work with you to increase your AOV (Average Order Value), Customer LTV (Lifetime Value), and brand awareness. 

This way, you can shortcut years of learning how to market and focus on creating awesome products and engaging with people who love them.
